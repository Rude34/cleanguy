title: "Bluetooth Headphones Connected But No Sound on Windows"
description: "Solve no-audio issues when Bluetooth headset is paired but audio output is wrong."
category: "Audio"
minutes: 4
updatedAt: "2026-03-20"
---

When Bluetooth headphones connect but sound still plays from speakers:

1. Open **Settings > System > Sound > Output**.
2. Select your Bluetooth headset as the active output device.
3. In app settings, choose the same headset for output.
4. Disconnect and reconnect Bluetooth from **Settings > Bluetooth & devices**.
5. Remove the headset and pair again if needed.

Tip: Disable unused output devices temporarily to avoid automatic switching.
