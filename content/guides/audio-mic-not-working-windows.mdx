title: "Microphone Not Working on Windows? Quick Fix Steps"
description: "Fix microphone input issues on Windows with privacy, device, and driver checks."
category: "Audio"
minutes: 5
updatedAt: "2026-03-20"
---

If your mic is not detected or no one can hear you, follow these checks:

1. Go to **Settings > Privacy & security > Microphone** and enable microphone access.
2. Open **Settings > System > Sound > Input** and select the correct microphone.
3. Click **Input volume** and set it between 70-100.
4. In **Control Panel > Sound > Recording**, set your mic as **Default Device**.
5. Restart the app (Discord/Zoom/Teams) and test again.

If still not working, update or reinstall the audio input driver from Device Manager.
