description: "Fix Zoom camera not found errors with privacy settings, driver checks, and app conflicts."
category: "Apps"

updatedAt: "2026-03-20"
---

If Zoom cannot find your camera:

1. Enable camera access in Windows Privacy settings.
2. Close other apps using the camera (Teams, browser, OBS).
3. In Zoom settings, pick the correct camera device.
4. Update camera driver from Device Manager.
5. Restart Zoom and test video.

If external webcam still fails, test another USB port and cable.
