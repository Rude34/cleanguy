---
title: "Fix Slow Boot Time on Windows"
description: "Speed up startup by reducing boot load, optimizing startup apps, and checking disk health."
category: "Performance"
minutes: 5
updatedAt: "2026-03-20"
---

Long startup time usually comes from heavy startup apps and service load.

1. Open Task Manager > Startup Apps.
2. Disable high-impact apps you don’t need.
3. Check free disk space (keep at least 15-20%).
4. Enable Fast Startup in Power Options.
5. Reboot and measure boot time again.

If boot is still very slow, check SSD/HDD health with manufacturer tools.
