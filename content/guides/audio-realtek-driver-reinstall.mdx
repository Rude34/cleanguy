If your Realtek audio stops working after update:

1. Open **Device Manager > Sound, video and game controllers**.
2. Right-click **Realtek(R) Audio** > **Uninstall device**.
3. Check **Delete the driver software** if available.
4. Restart your PC.
5. Install latest Realtek driver from your laptop/motherboard support page.
6. Restart again and test speaker + headphone output.

Avoid using unknown driver-pack websites; use official manufacturer sources.
