title: "Microsoft Office Activation Error on Windows"
description: "Resolve common Office activation failures by checking account, license sync, and service status."
category: "Apps"
minutes: 6
updatedAt: "2026-03-20"
---

If Office asks for activation repeatedly:

1. Confirm you are signed in with the licensed Microsoft account.
2. Check internet connection and date/time sync.
3. Open any Office app > Account > Sign out/sign in.
4. Run Office Quick Repair from Apps settings.
5. If needed, run Online Repair.

For business licenses, verify subscription status in Microsoft 365 admin portal.
