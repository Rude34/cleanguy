Crackling sound often comes from format mismatch or enhancements.

1. Open **Control Panel > Sound > Playback**.
2. Select your device > **Properties > Advanced**.
3. Change Default Format to **16 bit, 44100 Hz** or **16 bit, 48000 Hz**.
4. In **Enhancements** tab, disable all sound enhancements.
5. In **Spatial sound**, set to **Off** and retest.

Also update chipset and audio drivers from your laptop/motherboard manufacturer site.
