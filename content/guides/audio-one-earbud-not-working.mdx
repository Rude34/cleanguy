title: "One Earbud Not Working on Windows: Left/Right Channel Fix"
description: "Fix single-sided audio by checking balance settings, mono mode, and device reset."
category: "Audio"
minutes: 4
updatedAt: "2026-03-20"
---

If audio only comes from one side:

1. Go to **Settings > System > Sound > Output > Device properties**.
2. Check **Left/Right balance** and set both to 100.
3. Disable **Mono audio** in accessibility sound settings.
4. Reconnect the earbuds and test with another app.
5. Test the earbuds on a phone to rule out hardware failure.

If one side still has no sound on all devices, the earbud may need replacement.
